And we have the solution to recruiting. Regardless of industry, vocation or talent pool, Collar.Solutions can ensure your recruiting operation stays at the forefront of your competitors by consistently supplying the most skilled individuals the market can bare.

The right candidates, every time.

Recruiters understand how many pieces of a puzzle need to be brought together in order to make a hire. They also understand that the most difficult piece of the puzzle is something out of their control - finding a candidate who’s both qualified for the job and is ready to make a career move. Recruitment professionals often call candidates off of one list, hoping to get a hold of just one of the individuals they have pre-qualified for the job for which they’re sourcing. Not only do most people not answer their phones or read emails, but these candidate source lists are often overworked, and the potential candidates are tired of being offered a job when they aren’t currently realizing limitations within their current roles.

So what happens when recruiters who were tired of the old process mesh with experts in big data, artificial intelligence and advanced algorithmic matching?

Collar happened.

Big Data

By utilizing over 1000 source verified databases and data sets, Collar is able to identify a wider breadth of individuals whom are well-qualified to perform the job for which you are sourcing.

These datasets deliver behavioral and psychographic patterns and associations that help syphon unique skillsets into your hiring funnel. By computationally widening the talent pool, we are able to deliver more candidates who are not only qualified to perform the job at hand, but also ready to make a career move.

AI

Our proprietary technology was developed with the ability to learn from past experience. Once the system has identified specific individuals to fulfill a hiring need, the technology ‘watches’ as certain individuals make their way through the hiring funnel, and as others syphon off.

The system then analyzes the behaviors, psychographics, and demographics relevant to each candidate so that it can make a better-informed prediction during the next hiring round.

Matching

Once the data has been cleansed for import, Collar uses an advanced algorithmic matching technique in order to contact your ideal candidate pools. SMS.

These candidates can be sent real-time messages on social platforms such as LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram or Twitter, or SMS.

By accessing your audience where they spend a majority of their time (social media), Collar is able to procure candidates with the right skillsets at the right time.
