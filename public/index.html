Predictive Hiring

Collar Solutions is the innovation, creation, and marketing arm of the Collar System. We deploy big data, artificial intelligence, and continuous market engagement to provide predictive hiring.

By automating high-volume recruiting tasks such as sourcing and screening, Collar Solutions proactively matches employers with larger pools of qualified, interested, and affordable candidates.
