Predictive Solutions

Collar Solutions is the innovation, creation, and marketing arm of the Collar System, which deploys big data, artificial intelligence, and continuous market engagement to provide predictive solutions.

By intelligently automating conventional processes Collar Solutions optimizes targeted sourcing, enabling organizations to save time, money, and successfully connect with more people.

Collar Solutions provides predictive solutions to legacy industries such as staffing and recruiting.
